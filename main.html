Places Of AGRA

AGRA FORT

Agra Fort is a historical fort in the city of Agra in India. It was the main residence of the emperors of the Mughal Dynasty till 1638, when the capital was shifted from Agra to Delhi. The Agra fort is a UNESCO World Heritage site. It is about 2.5 km northwest of its more famous sister monument, the Taj Mahal.

TAJ MAHAL

This magnificent white marble structure was constructed by the Mughal Emperor, Shah Jahan, in the memory of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Besides being a symbol of eternal love, this monument perfectly combines Mughal, Turkish, Persian, and Indian architectural styles.

FATHEPUR SIKRI

Located a little away from Agra, Fatehpur Sikri is the ghost city that comprised of various important monuments. It is supposed to be abandoned by the people because of the lack of water. Fatehpur Sikri is a charming city complex commissioned by the great Mughal Emperor, Akbar.
